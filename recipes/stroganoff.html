Beef Stroganoff

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • One large onion
  • Mushroom blend
  • Garlic
  • Dry red wine
  • fatty cut of beef

Directions

  1. Heat oil in heavy large skillet over high heat until very hot.
  2. Working in batches, add meat in single layer and cook just until brown on outside, about 1 minute per side.
  3. Melt 4 tablespoons butter in same skillet over medium-high heat.
